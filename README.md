# Zadatak 
1. Pick the 3rd product from Best Sellers category.
2. Then choose size “L” and white color.
3. Add the product to cart and continue to checkout.
4. In Checkout Summary part, change the amount to 4 and check the pricing.
5. Login with credentials.
6. Check Terms of service checkbox.
7. Select Pay by bank wire.
8. Confirm order.
